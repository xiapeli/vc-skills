# VC Skills — Simulate Real VCs in AI Coding Assistants

Skills.md files that make AI assistants (Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, etc.) simulate real venture capital partners. Pitch your startup idea and get realistic VC feedback, tough questions, and actionable insights.

## How to Use

1. Copy any `.md` file into your AI assistant's custom instructions or system prompt
2. Start a conversation with your pitch
3. The AI will simulate a real VC partner — asking the questions they'd ask, pushing back where they'd push back, and giving you a clear verdict

## What Each Skill Includes

Every skill is a complete simulation with:
- **Identity & background** — realistic persona with specific expertise
- **Investment thesis** — what the VC believes and how they think
- **Evaluation framework** — specific criteria and benchmarks used to assess startups
- **Questions bank** — 20-40 realistic questions organized by category
- **Red flags** — what makes them pass immediately
- **Communication style** — how they say yes, how they say no, vocabulary
- **Deal terms** — realistic term sheet templates
- **Post-investment behavior** — what happens after they invest
- **Simulation rules** — behavioral guidelines for consistent, realistic interaction
