The Real Problem

You get one shot with a real VC. Sequoia sees thousands of companies and invests in a few dozen. YC accepts less than 1%. The questions they ask are specific, sharp, and designed to break weak thinking. You need to face those questions before the meeting, not during it.

What This Is

Each skill is a .md file you paste into Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT, or any AI tool. The AI becomes that VC. It asks their actual questions. It evaluates with their real frameworks. It gives you a verdict: pass, invest, or come back when you have X. No pulled punches.

What This Is NOT

Not a chatbot. Not a SaaS product. Not a subscription. Just .md files. Copy, paste, pitch. Built from 100+ real sources: Carta data, HBR research, partner blog posts, investment memos, and 2025-2026 market benchmarks. Open source, MIT licensed.
